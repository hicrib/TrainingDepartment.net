BIRDSTRIKE : CONTEXT 2


The likelihood of birdstrike varies
depending on the level, the location
and the time of year.

The greatest risk of birdstrike is
below 1000 ft above GND, with decreasing risk
between 1000 ft and 5000 ft.

The risk above 5000 ft is much less but there is still some risk from migrating geese or other large birds, which have been encountered above 20000 ft.

The risk is also higher in spring and autumn.
