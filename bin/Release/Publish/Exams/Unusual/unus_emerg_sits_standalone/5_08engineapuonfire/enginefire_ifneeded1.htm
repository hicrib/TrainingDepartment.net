   ENGINE/APU ON FIRE : IF NEEDED 1


IF NEEDED:
(This section applies particularly to light aircraft where the pilot may be inexperienced.
Commercial pilots will already be familiar with these procedures.)

Assist by informing the pilot about:

  • next suitable aerodrome

  • aerodrome details as soon as possible: RWY in use, length, surface, elevation

  • WX information at landing aerodrome: wind, visibility, ceiling, QNH

For private pilots assist by enquiring if

  • fuel selector is off
  • they have checked mixture (fuel cut off??)
  • fuel pump is off
  • cabin heating and venting is off
  • they have checked their glide speed limit
