COURSE AIMS

Welcome to Eurocontrol's on-line unusual / emergency situations training course, intended for Air Traffic Controller students who wish to learn or Air Traffic Controllers who wish to revise. The aims of the course are:

  • To enable users to learn at their own pace, at a time and place which suits their needs
  • To facilitate learning by the inclusion of interactive features and self assessment tools
  • To aid understanding of the nature of each incident by provision of background information
    about how it arises and how it affects the aircraft and crew
  • To build on the 15 proposed checklists for emergencies and unusual incidents
    which form part of the EATMP deliverable "controller training in the handling of unusual incidents".

The EATMP emergency checklists relate to the different controller domains Area, Approach and Aerodrome. They specify both urgent and essential actions which ATC should take immediately in the event of an emergency, and additional, supplementary actions, which ATC should be ready to carry out if the situation requires. This structure has been reflected in the lessons. The checklists also draw controllers' attention to other incidents which may result from a particular emergency.
