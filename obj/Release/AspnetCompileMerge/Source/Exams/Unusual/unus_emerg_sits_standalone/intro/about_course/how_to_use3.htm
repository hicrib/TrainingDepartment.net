HOW TO USE: ASSIST

EATMP has recommended the acronym 'ASSIST' to help controllers remember a simple set of guidelines to be followed initially in any emergency or unusual situation. This acronym is also included in all the Checklists.

Roll your mouse over the letters to view what they stand for and click on them for further definition.
