HOW TO USE: Emergency topics
 


Emergencies are complex in nature. When one emergency occurs it may trigger other emergencies. The current emergency may in turn have been triggered by another emergency.

Use the panel on the left to select one of the 15 emergency topics to study. Try clicking on the buttons now! You will see how clicking on one emergency may also highlight others.

To illustrate this, when you select an emergency, any other related emergencies will light up . By 'related' we mean other emergencies which ATC can expect as a result of the current one.

When you click an emergency topic, the lesson content will be loaded into this right hand panel, with a button bar at the top to help you navigate within that particular topic.
