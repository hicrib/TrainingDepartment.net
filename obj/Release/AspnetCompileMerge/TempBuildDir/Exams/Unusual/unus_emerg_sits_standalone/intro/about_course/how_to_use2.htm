HOW TO USE: Navigation within emergency topic
ICING

The buttons above will let you jump to the start of each section in the learning material. You might find these helpful for revision.

The sections named, 'Expect', 'Remember' and 'If needed' reflect the headings used in the Checklists. The section 'Context' provides some background information on the emergency to aid your understanding and awareness. The 'Checklist' button will open a copy of the actual checklist in a 'pop-up' window, which will close automatically as soon as you click outside of it.

Try pointing your mouse at the section headings. Note how they change colour. A red highlight means "watch out, danger!" These are the sections highlighting what might go wrong. A green highlight means "here are some ways you can help!" These are the sections about steps ATC can take to help.
